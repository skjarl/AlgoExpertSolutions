Theta n = worst case complexity
O(1) = constant time
O(log n) = logarithmic time (divide & conquer algorithms, bubble sort)
O(n) = linear time
O(n log n) = linearithmic time
O(n^2) = quadratic time (bubble sort)
O(n^3) = cubic time
O(2^n) = exponential time (slow, brute force algorithms)
O(n!) = factorial time (slowest, traveling salesman)
